About Youngest Modern Farmer

We are a youth-led movement transforming agriculture through innovation, training, and a strong commitment to food safety. Our mission is to make farming a viable, modern, and respected career for the next generation.

Vision

To make farming the most attractive and profitable venture for youth, while ensuring safe and nutritious food for communities.

Mission

Empower youth with practical skills, access to technology, and market connections so they can build sustainable agribusinesses.
